    One  fine evening in the year 1983 some youth of this locality headed by S/Shri  Swapan Chakraborty, Dibyendu Sanyal and others     while one bridegroom party came  to marry one of our elder sister expressed his idea for some financial help for  forming of a club in this locality and accordingly the bridegroom party was  approached for the same and with great interest they responded and donated   Rs. 500/- for forming a club in this  locality. In the same year one of our well known youth suddenly electrocuted.  In memory of this beloved youth along with the donation given by the bridegroom  party our club named as   JATIYA SHAKTI SANGHA AND PATHAGAR   came into existence  and a small plot along the Champasari Main road measuring 4 kathas was arranged  with the positive attitude and help of Roy Jethamashai , National Award Winner  Teacher, Late Shailendra Kumar Sen and Shri Arabinda Biswas, the then  President, Siliguri-Naxalbari Panchyat Samity.
