Apart from the social activities a special  attention are always on in the sports segment for encouraging the children of  this area.

- A  Running cricket tournament is being held in every year wherein most of the  prominent clubs of Siliguri, Malda , Coochbehar and Alipurduar are  participated.

- Table  Tennis tournament is being host under the banner of Siliguri District Table  Tennis Association & Siliguri Municipal Corporation.

- Table  Tennis coaching is imparted under the guidance of eminent tennis player of the  district to about 100 children of the area.

- Bharat  Natyam dance are being taught by the renouned Bharat Natyam Dancer of the  Siliguri on weekly basis.
