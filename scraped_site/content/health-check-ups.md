# Health Check-ups

Source: http://siligurijss.org/health-check-ups.html

|
                 | Sports  || Social  || Library  || Health Check-ups

- An  important steps has been taken by organizing frequent health check viz. Free  Eye Testing with the help of prominent doctors of Siliguri in favour of the  poor people of the area as well as senior citizens who are not in a position to  afford costly treatment.

- Blood  Donations camp are being organized on each and every Red Letter Day wherein  most of the members of the club are donated their blood for the poor people of  the society.

- Poor patients are  being helped financially for treatment in the outside in terms of arrangement  of Rly/Air tickets by the club as well as comprehensive information about the  hospital/treatment centre.
