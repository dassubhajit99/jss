# Future Plan

Source: http://siligurijss.org/future-plan.html

Future activities -

 Though, our club has already associated with numerous social programme being organized by various Government and Non-Government agencies from time to time , even though some added permanent privileges are planned in coming years, details of which are appended.

a)  Library for poor students - ( Free of Cost )

            Since our club activities are spreaded over to a large area with high concentration of population which includes number of poor meritorious students who are potential in their education but could not succeed due to paucity of required education facilities , a Library is being designed to have at-least 5000 books of various standard including different reference books which will benefit the maximum number of poor students of this area.

 b)                Free Health Check-up camps ( with outside doctors)

            We are already organizing frequent free health check-up camps  with the doctors of good repute of Siliguri . However, efforts are being on to make available of doctors , who are visited Siliguri on occasion basis.

 c)                 With the completion of club house

           A portion of the same will be opened for using it as Community Hall with minimum cleanliness charge. This will ease the poor people to organize their domestic functions with minimum cost.
