 Plan for   making the surroundings green -

 With the increasing trend   of global warming effect, the time has come to prevent the effect to a great   extent. Accordingly, every year regular campaign for plantation in a large   extent is being organized under which different type of plants are being planted   in the area and seminars are being planned amongst the locales to aware the bad   effect of cutting of trees , good effect of plantation etc.

All the school premises of   the area are being planted by the members of the club and the school authorities   are entrusted to look after the growth / maintenance of the plants with mutual   understanding.

Unauthorized cutting of   trees, if noticed, by the club members are being prevented with the help of   Forest Department. A new plan for plantation of medicinal plants are being   thought of and various Ayurvedic Medicine Manufacturers, especially , in   Uttarakhand are being contacted for this purpose. Club premises are planted with   different varieties of valuable fruit trees.
